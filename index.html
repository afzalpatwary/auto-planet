What is motor insurance?

Car insurance also called vehicle insurance is the safeguarding of all road vehicles including cars, trucks, cars, and motorcycles.

Car insurance is a financial guarantee protecting your vehicle from unforeseen risks. It protects the policyholder against physical damage, bodily injuries, property damage, and even damage incurred to a third party. Additionally, it protects you from vehicle theft, and natural or weather calamities.

The terms and conditions associated with motor insurance are different in every country. As per the UAE mandate, every car owner must have at least third-party insurance.

Benefits of car insurance

  • Safeguard against car loss or car damage
  • Unlimited third-party liability including injury claims and death
  • Indemnity against third-party property damage claims
  • Financial liability protection
  • Availability of add-ons for extra protection cover
  • Financial liability protection
  • Road -side assistance and towing for worry free drive

Types of car insurance

Comprehensive car insurance

Comprehensive car insurance along with protecting you from third-party claims also safeguards your vehicle from damages arising out of calamities- both man-made and natural including fire and theft. With add-ons, you are also cover personal accident costs and the cost of repairs required on account of the car owner’s miscalculation.

Only third-party liability car insurance

As per the UAE federal law, every car owner must have third-party liability insurance. It is the most basic type of car insurance. This indemnified the car owner against any third-party liabilities. So, your insurance provider will cover all bodily damages to a third party.

Filling a claim

In the event of an accident, the policy owner or the car driver
must first notify the concerned official authorities. And within
sustainable time provide the insurance provider with all required
documents and details relating to the accidents.

Information of any lawsuit, investigations, or findings relating to
the accidents, must also be made known to your insurance
provider as soon as possible.

In case of criminal activities such as theft, which are covered
under the policy, insured for their protection, must promptly
notify the concerned authorities and the company at
the earliest.
